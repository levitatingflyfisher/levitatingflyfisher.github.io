OpenHearth

Calm, private tools for the life of a home.

Software should be like a good kitchen table — useful, durable, and yours. OpenHearth builds small, local-first apps for reading, learning, and family life. Everything runs on your device. Install any of them like an app, right from your browser.

Local-first No ads, no tracking No account needed Open source

The apps

OpenHearth is free and open. Built in the open on GitHub.

Each app is a Progressive Web App — open it in your phone's browser and choose “Install” / “Add to Home screen” to keep it offline. Android users can also sideload the APK.
